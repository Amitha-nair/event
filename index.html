The First Global Virtual Symposium

The initiative is the first Global PES Young Professional Virtual Symposium organized by IEEE PES Young Professionals Kerala Chapter with a mission to leverage the interaction of IEEE PES Young professionals all over the world. The event procures a platform to share their experience, to build a decisive network and to provide the latest technical knowledge to its members through workshops, competitions and several reliable talk sessions. The aim is to help the Young Professionals in elevating their professional image, expanding their global network by exchanging the sprouted ideas to the whole world. The symposium includes country-wise technical competitions, panel discussions relevant topics, skill building sessions and so on.
